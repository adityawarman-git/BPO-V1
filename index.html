The Founders Blueprint (Core Team)

CORE TEAM & SCOPE OF RESPONSIBILITY

Company Overview: A newly established Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) company based in Jakarta, specializing in Customer Service and Telemarketing. Offering Full Solution, Labour Supply, and Infrastructure Only services. Looking for highly driven, entrepreneurial BPO veterans to join the founding core team and build this company from the ground up.

1. SENIOR ΒΡΟ ADVISOR (Part-Time / Retainer)

Role Overview: This role will be the strategic compass for the executive team, leveraging his/her extensive network and industry expertise to guide go-to-market strategy, ensure legal compliance, and unlock Enterprise-level opportunities.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Open doors and facilitate C-level introductions to prospective Enterprise clients (Banking, Fintech, Telco, E-commerce).
  • Draft, review, and finalize Master Service Agreements (MSA) and SLA frameworks.
  • Develop comprehensive Business Continuity Plans (BCP) and Disaster Recovery Plans (DRP).
  • Guide the IT and Ops teams toward ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 readiness.
  • Act as a strategic mentor to the founding core team, conducting monthly executive reviews to ensure the business is scaling safely.
  • Advise on the legal structure of outsourcing contracts (PKWT) to mitigate industrial relations risks under Indonesian Labor Laws.

Requirements:

  • 10-15+ years of Senior Leadership experience (VP/C-Level) at a Top Tier BPO in Indonesia.
  • Deep understanding of Indonesian labor laws (UU Cipta Kerja) regarding outsourcing and contract structuring.
  • Active and warm connections with decision-makers (C-Level/VP) in target industries.
  • Proven track record of scaling BPO operations from zero, or successfully executing a turnaround strategy for underperforming BPO sites.
  • Strong command of compliance frameworks, specifically ISO 9001 and ISO 27001 implementations.

Compensation: Monthly Retainer Fee + Lucrative Success Fee (Percentage of Gross Margin per closed Enterprise deal).

2. VP OF BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT & SALES (Full-Time)

Role Overview: This role is a primary rainmaker. His/her mission is to secure the first batch of B2B clients and establish a scalable revenue pipeline. He/she will own the pricing strategy and client acquisition process.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Drive monthly and quarterly revenue targets (MRR) through aggressive B2B sales.
  • Develop commercial rate cards for Per Seat/FTE, Per Minute, and Pay-per-Performance models.
  • Manage the end-to-end sales cycle, from lead generation and pitching to contract negotiation and closing.
  • Act as the primary Key Account Manager for early clients, conducting Quarterly Business Reviews (QBR) to secure contract renewals and upselling opportunities.
  • Implement and maintain a CRM system to accurately track the sales pipeline, lead conversion rates, and revenue forecasts for the CEO/Board.

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of B2B Sales experience in the BPO, Contact Center, or SaaS Omnichannel industry.
  • Mandatory: Must possess a strong existing pipeline or 'Bring Your Own Client' (BYOC) capability.
  • Proven track record of closing and managing enterprise accounts of 50+ seats.
  • Hands-on experience navigating B2B enterprise procurement processes and e-Tender portals (e.g., Ariba, SAP, LPSE).
  • "Roll-up-your-sleeves" startup mentality: capable of building pitch decks, calculating complex pricing margins (Excel proficiency), and pitching independently.

Compensation: Competitive Base Salary + Uncapped Recurring Commission per active seat + ESOP (Employee Stock Ownership Plan) upon hitting targets.

3. VP OF BPO OPERATIONS (Full-Time)

Role Overview: This role is the commander of the production floor. He/she will translate client SLAs into operational reality, ensuring agents deliver top-tier performance while maintaining healthy margins and low turnover.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Set up production floor SOPs, Quality Assurance (QA) frameworks, and escalation matrices from scratch.
  • Drive mass hiring, training modules, and workforce management (WFM/Shift scheduling).
  • Strictly monitor and optimize core BPO metrics: SLA, AHT, CSAT, Shrinkage, and Occupancy Rate.
  • Lead client-facing operational syncs (Weekly/Monthly Business Reviews) to report on SLA performance and action plans.
  • Manage production floor P&L directly by strictly controlling overtime (OT) budgets, optimizing headcount efficiency, and minimizing agent attrition rates.

Requirements:

  • 5-7+ years as an Operations Manager or Site Manager managing 100+ seats in a BPO environment.
  • Highly data-driven with exceptional analytical skills for workforce forecasting (Erlang C calculation, Shrinkage, Occupancy).
  • Deep familiarity with COPC (Customer Operations Performance Center) standards and frameworks.
  • Hands-on experience managing Omnichannel operations (Voice, WhatsApp API, Email, Social Media CRM like Zendesk, Genesys, or local SaaS).
  • Builder mindset: willing to personally draft SOPs, conduct initial agent interviews, and handle floor escalations in the early stages of the company.

Compensation: Competitive Base Salary + Quarterly Performance Bonus (tied to SLA achievement and low attrition) + ESOP.

4. HEAD OF IT & INFRASTRUCTURE (Full-Time)

Role Overview: This role is the architect of the digital backbone. He/she will build and maintain a cost-effective, highly secure, and scalable IT infrastructure for both internal operations and 'Infrastructure Only' clients.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Deploy and maintain Cloud PBX/Dialers (e.g., viciDial, Asterisk) and integrate Omnichannel SaaS CRM.
  • Manage hardware deployment, LAN/WAN architecture, and Dual ISP failover systems to guarantee 99.9% uptime.
  • Enforce strict data security protocols (USB blocking, firewalls, site restrictions) to meet financial industry standards.
  • Handle direct vendor management and procurement negotiations (ISPs, hardware suppliers, software licenses) to ensure CAPEX/OPEX efficiency.
  • Establish and manage the internal IT Helpdesk/Ticketing system to provide rapid troubleshooting for production floor agents.

Requirements:

  • 5+ years as an IT/Network Administrator, specifically within a Contact Center/BPO environment.
  • Hands-on experience with open-source dialing technologies (viciDial, Asterisk, FreePBX).
  • [NEW] Strong networking expertise: hands-on configuration of Mikrotik/Cisco routers, VPNs, and VLANs.
  • Proficient in REST API integrations (connecting Cloud Dialers with third-party CRM systems).
  • Solid understanding of ISO 27001 data security compliance, encompassing physical and logical security measures (firewalls, endpoint protection).

Compensation: Competitive Base Salary + Quarterly Uptime Bonus + ESOP.
